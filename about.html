About Me

I'm just a typical college student, much like everyone else, who enjoys engaging in activities common among young people, such as playing video games and using social media. As I find myself immersed in my daily social media habits, I often wonder which social media platform is currently the most popular, mostly based on their user counts. So this little site is my effort to provide some "top of the iceberg" insights into that topic.
